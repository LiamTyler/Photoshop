\subsection{Detailed Description}
Superclass for all kernals. 

Kernals are two dimensional arrays that are used in convolution filters. A kernal is centered over a pixel, and a weighted sum is performed between all the pixels beneath the kernal after the pixel gets multiplied by the amount in that element of the kernal.
